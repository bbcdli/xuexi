One package contains 8g. 

Each time 1g jiaomu, 200ml rice (temporature 30 degree)
1. put the rice in water for 6 hours (ji ben qing shui hou)
2. use dampf to cook the rice, place a cloth above normal rice, then put the special rice on the cloth.
3. shower the rice. until the special rice not klebig and meanwhile, no water can be seen
4. fill the finished rice into a glass container. put the rice and jiuqu layer for layer into the container. evenly distribute the rice, totally should be 3 to 5 layers. In the end, dig a hole in the middle of the rice and put the rest jiuqu into the hole.
5. wait for 24 hours, rice wine should be appear, 36 hour should be finally finished.
